Method: GUIDANCE

Population size: 19,428 msa alignments from CDD after filtering

Random sample size: 100 msa alignments, very diverse dimensions (number of sequences, length of sequences)

Random sequences: from Unprot. Sequence length exactly matches the Average MSA seq. len. (gaps removed)

Number of random sequence: as many as needed to get close to the desired random ratio. The actual random ratio is bound to be less then the desired ratio by within 0.05 (5% of residues are noise). Ratio of foreign residues after injection is X where (desired_rand_ratio - 0.05) < X ≤ desired_rand_ratio

Computational Labour: (5 samples) × (1 original group + 10 injections of foreign sequences) × (100 original msa alignments) × (GUIDANCE score by 100 msa guide-tree BP tree reconstruction and re-alignments). Total: 550,000 re-alignments with MAFFT.
